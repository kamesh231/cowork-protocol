# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

COWORK Protocol is built by people from different disciplines — designers, product managers, engineers, researchers, and domain experts. This diversity is our strength. We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, constructive experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Do:**
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Ground feedback in real-world experience ("I've seen this break because...")
- Respect that different domains have different vocabulary for similar concepts
- Focus on the ideas, not the people presenting them
- Accept that your perspective is one of many, and the best protocols emerge from synthesis

**Don't:**
- Use dismissive language ("that's obvious" / "that would never work")
- Derail discussions with product pitches or self-promotion
- Assume bad intent when someone disagrees with your position
- Gatekeep based on job title, company size, or technical background

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.
